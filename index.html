Tri-Nhu Do is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Resilient Machine Learning Institute (ReMI) at École de technologie supérieure (ÉTS), University of Quebec, Montreal, Canada, where his research interests include wireless communications and AI/ML. Prior to this, he received his bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from the Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology in Vietnam in 2012, and his master's and Ph.D. in Electronics and Computer Engineering from Hongik University in South Korea in 2015 and 2018, respectively.
